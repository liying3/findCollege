		What the School Says...Mount Vernon Nazarene University is a private, four-year, intentionally Christian teaching university for traditional-age students, graduate students, and working adults. With a 400-acre main campus in Mount Vernon, Ohio, and several convenient Graduate and Professional Studies locations throughout the state, MVNU emphasizes academic excellence, spiritual growth, and service to community and church. MVNU offers an affordable education to more than 2,600 students from 26 states and seven countries/U.S. territories.
What the School Says...At MVNU, caring admissions counselors and financial representatives are dedicated to helping you through your decision process, finding scholarship opportunities, and answering all of your important questions. As a prospective student, you are assigned to a specific admissions counselor, based on your geographical location, and this person quickly becomes an advocate for you. What sets the MVNU admissions team apart is their commitment to relationships  each counselor knows who you are. This team is genuinely concerned for your best interests as you make this important step in your life!
What the School Says...With private education, money is often the big issue. So, you'll be comforted to know that MVNU is consistently ranked as one of the most affordable private universities in Ohio. Plus, 98 percent of all MVNU students receive some form of financial assistance. Between university scholarships, private funding offered exclusively to MVNU students, and expert assistance through the government financial aid process, you won't have to worry about how to pay for college! Instead, you’'l find an incredible value at an affordable price! 
What the School Says...With an average student/professor ratio of 15 to 1, each student learns at a relational level, as professors are committed to student success in all areas of their college experience. Professors make themselves available to their students in ways that would be impossible at public universities. Additional help is offered through peer mentorship, and academic counselors are available to ensure that students have every opportunity to excel academically. MVNU's liberal arts education also offers a unique partnership with culture and a Christian worldview that challenges and shapes each student as they enter their field.
What the School Says...Located on 400 acres of rural farmland, but just a short drive to the big city, MVNU offers the best of both worlds. Plus, you'll find big-time college athletics, a wide variety of intramurals, campus events, cultural performances, or a pick-up game of disc golf. Yeah, we have our own course. Most important, MVNU fosters the kinds of relationships that will extend beyond the four years you spend on campus. Each event cultivates relationships between your peers, faculty, and staff, while challenging you to think, grow, and discover who it is you've been created to be.
A Message from the SchoolMVNU students are challenged to partner academic excellence with a mission: to change the world with the love of Christ! Here, hearts are stirred to compassion just as minds are stirred to understanding. This momentum has global effects, resulting in thousands of inspiring stories from students and alumni alike. Whether the mission field becomes their workplace, their neighborhood, or their family, MVNU students and graduates across the world recognize their responsibility and embrace their call to ministry.
