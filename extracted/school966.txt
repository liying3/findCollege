What the School Says...
Ranking consistently among the top universities, Case Western Reserve University, located in Cleveland, Ohio, offers unlimited opportunities for motivated students. To help you flourish, our faculty both challenges you and supports you. Our partnerships with world-class cultural, educational, and scientific institutions ensure that your education extends beyond the classroom. 
What the School Says...
Motivated students thrive at Case Western, where renowned academics, research, and experiential learning offer all undergraduates the chance to explore their interests, expand their knowledge, and grow into active, productive citizens. We review each application for admission individually, carefully taking into consideration each student's academic background, life experiences, and interests. Applicants will receive e-mail reminders of deadlines throughout the year. We offer a series of open houses for prospective students at all phases of the application process.
What the School Says...
A great education is a great investment. Case Western's experiential learning approach puts our graduates ahead of their peers in experience, connections to employers, and in many cases, starting salaries. Case Western offers a variety of need- and merit-based aid to qualifying applicants.
What the School Says...
Case Western offers more than 75 majors and minors in an intimate college setting at the forefront of higher education and innovation. Our approach to courses of study is intellectually diverse and academically flexible. Our single-door admission policy means that once you're admitted to Case Western, you can major in any of our programs, or double and even triple in several of them.  Our emphasis on experiential learning comes in many forms. Students can participate in undergraduate research, immerse themselves in other cultures through our study abroad programs, engage in clinical work as a nursing student, or work for an engineering firm through a co-op program.
What the School Says...
It's the best of both worlds: Our 155-acre, park-like campus is located within a larger, extremely vibrant urban community. Our University Circle neighborhood boasts an unsurpassed concentration of cultural, medical, and educational institutions (more than 50 in one square mile). All first-year students live together in one of four themed residential colleges that help them adjust to university life and  learn about resources available throughout Cleveland. Cedar College focuses on the arts, Juniper on world culture, Magnolia on leadership, and Mistletoe on service. The campus is also home to more than 100 student organizations, including professional, Greek, social, ethnic, faith, and service groups.
What the School Says...
SAGES (Seminar Approach to General Education and Scholarship) is woven into all four years of your Case Western career. It is a springboard into new ways of thinking, new methods of communicating, and opportunities for leadership and intellectual growth. The program brings together students of all majors, faculty from all over the university, and community leaders from northeast Ohio. Case Western is known internationally as a leading research institution and welcomes a diverse student body from 80 countries around the world.
