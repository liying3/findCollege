What the School Says...
California State University, East Bay is known for award winning programs, expert instruction, small classes, a highly personalized learning environment, and more than 100 career-focused fields of study. Visit our website to tour our two scenic campuses: one in Hayward, overlooking San Francisco Bay, and the other in Concord, near Mt. Diablo. Cal State East Bay is where all your possibilities come into view. Cal State East Bay is known for its highly prepared graduates who are in demand by regional and national employers. Students enjoy a full range of academic and career support services, a state-of-the-art recreation and wellness center, on-campus living and dining, exciting athletic teams, and more than 100 student clubs and organizations. Questions? Call the Welcome Center at (510) 885-2556.
What the School Says...
Cal State East Bay is a popular destination for undergraduate and graduate students and students transferring from community college. We accept applications year-round. Visit our website to learn more about our admission requirements, our WUE program for students from western states, international student admission, and study abroad program. Pre-admission counseling is offered to all prospective students by appointment at our two campus locations or at area admission events. Visit our website to find out how to make an appointment at the Hayward Campus Welcome Center or the Concord Campus Academic Service Center. International students should call (510) 885-7571. 
What the School Says...
At Cal State East Bay, we recognize that even an affordable college education can be a significant financial commitment. That's why each year we offer our students more than $100 million in grants, loans, scholarships, and other financial aid. The Office of Financial Aid staff is dedicated to helping you finance your education and provides resources and information to make college accessible for all. 
What the School Says...
Cal State East Bay offers a vigorous academic course of study with a real-world curriculum to prepare you for a lifetime of personal achievement and career success. Our professors are engaged with students on a personal basis, and the campus offers a friendly environment for an enriched learning experience. Rated a "Best in the West" University by the Princeton Review, Cal State East Bay is a great place to earn your degree, prepare for your future, and make lifelong friends. Visit our website to learn more about our academic programs.
What the School Says...
"Fun? You've got to be kidding!" said one student. "This is the San Francisco Bay Area. We have it all: clubs, restaurants, museums, parks, natural resources, the ocean, and skiing." Cal State East Bay offers an inspiring setting for personal growth, career exploration, academic achievement, and friendship. The East Bay offers many recreational, cultural, dining, and travel opportunities. On campus, you can meet students from around the world for a study group or to cheer on your favorite sports team. Enjoy a break at the Dining Commons or the many eateries at the Student Union. Take a fun class or catch a movie. Join a club or service organization or attend live music and theater events. Visit the Campus Life section of our website to learn about everything that's offered to you.
What the School Says...
Cal State East Bay is the most diverse of all the 23 campuses in the CSU system. The student body is a lively mix of undergraduate and graduate students from many backgrounds and cultures. Get some friends together and rent one of our beautiful furnished suites on campus. Our award winning freshman program matches you up with others who share your passions and major areas of study, resulting in greater success in your coursework.
