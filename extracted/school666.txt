What the School Says...
At Cal Poly, it is the University's mission to turn the students of today into the leaders of tomorrow. Located on California's central coast, Cal Poly is nestled between the beautiful Pacific Ocean and the vineyards of Edna Valley. Attracting the best and the brightest students from around the nation, Cal Poly provides the ultimate college experience. Ranked "Best in the West" for the 20th year in a row by U.S World News & Report, Cal Poly has a freshman return rate of over 90 percent, among the highest in the nation. With small class sizes, a safe campus environment, and hands on learning, it's no wonder Cal Poly continually has more qualified applicants than spots available. And when students aren't in the classroom, community and student life provide a vast wealth of recreation and entertainment suitable for a diverse range of interests. 
What the School Says...
Admission to Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo is highly competitive. Offering more than 60 bachelor's degrees in agriculture, architecture, business, science, math, liberal arts, and engineering, Cal Poly continues to have a higher number of qualified applicants than spots available. Cal Poly comprehensively reviews all applications, looking for individuals that not only demonstrate outstanding academic ability, but also those who have had a positive influence outside their high school classroom. 
What the School Says...
The Financial Aid Office helps students plan their finances and organize resources to cover college costs. Financial assistance is a resource designed to supplement the cost of education and is not intended to be used as a student's sole means of income. The types and amounts of aid Cal Poly awards or recommends are subject to availability of funds, enrollment status, and financial need. Cal Poly has a variety of financial aid programs available to assist with different types of need. Programs vary in eligibility criteria, award amounts, and repayment options.
What the School Says...
Ranked "Best in the West" for the 20th consecutive year in a row, Cal Poly's distinctive curriculum, anchored in a "learn by doing" philosophy, mixes rigorous academics with real-life, hands-on learning. Students are inspired and challenged to do more by Cal Poly faculty and staff, who not only teach, but act as mentors and role models to the students in their classrooms. Cal Poly's advanced academic curriculum prepares and provides students with a knowledge that sets our graduates apart from the rest. Post gradation, our highly sought-after graduates are able to immediately contribute to their fields, solve real problems, and make the world a better place.
What the School Says...
Campus life at Cal Poly represents the passion and vigor our students have for life itself. With a broad range of activities and opportunities for involvement, there is something for everyone! The newly expanded Recreation Center provides a great place for students to work out, swim, and recharge. Division I athletics and intramural sports provide students with a chance to compete at the level they desire. Our Theatre and Dance department encourages students to follow their inner thespian. Over 200 campus clubs, Greek life, a student run newspaper, a radio station, student government, and student leadership opportunities are some of the many activities Cal Poly student life has to offer. And, if you don't see a club that fits your interest, you can start one! 
What the School Says...
Cal Poly attracts the best and brightest students from around the nation. New freshman in 2012 boasted an average high school GPA of 3.87, with an average SAT score of 1268. Students choose Cal Poly for a quality education, advanced resources, and a wide array of opportunities. It is Cal Poly's mission that students succeed both in school and after graduation. The Cal Poly Academic Skills Center, Career Services, and Disability Resource Center are readily available to help students improve study skills, find a job after graduation, and much more.  In 2010, 62 percent of students obtained employment before graduation, 23 percent obtained employment within three months after graduation, and 15 percent obtained employment within nine months after graduation. These are some of the best employment rates in the country!
