What the School Says...
When you enroll at LIU Post, you become part of a close-knit community that is dedicated to helping you realize your full potential through a well-rounded liberal arts education. We are located just 27 miles west of the heart of NYC's Times Square, in Brookville, New York, a city ranked as one of the top places to live in America by Business Week. We enroll 8,700 undergraduate and graduate students from 42 states and 50 countries. Our 300-acre campus includes practice fields, tennis courts, and Long Island's only collegiate equestrian center. Students enjoy free access to the Pratt Recreation Center, an $18.5 million facility featuring basketball and volleyball courts, a heated swimming pool, 200-meter indoor running track, racquetball courses, and an exercise and fitness room. 
What the School Says...
The Admissions Committee evaluates your academic achievements and the curricular rigor of your academic history, your leadership potential, the depth of your participation in community service, extracurricular activities, and your overall interest in attending LIU Post. Freshmen applications for fall admission are accepted beginning December 1. 
What the School Says...
LIU Post is committed to making a quality, private education affordable. Eighty-five percent of our students receive financial assistance to help meet college expenses. LIU Post's experienced financial aid counselors will work closely with you and your family to make sure you receive the highest amount of aid possible. Financial aid is available in the form of scholarships, grants, and loans. Our free College Affordability and FAFSA Workshops are a great starting point. Held each winter, they are the ideal venues to help you determine the best way to plan the financing of your education.
What the School Says...
LIU Post is a doctoral-level institution of higher learning comprising five schools and colleges, nationally accredited degree programs, distinguished centers and institutes, a renowned research library, and a full-time faculty of over 300 scholars, researchers, scientists, artists, and humanitarians. LIU Post offers a full range of undergraduate and graduate programs in business, education, liberal arts and sciences, health professions and nursing, information and computer science, and visual and performing arts. Academic programs are accredited by the Commission of Higher Education of the Middle States Association of Colleges and Secondary Schools. Various other academic programs are accredited by professional organizations, signifying they meet the highest standards of educational quality. 
What the School Says...
Whether you live on campus or commute, LIU Post offers a variety of resources to help you get involved, meet academic and personal goals, excel academically, and start your career, all in a safe, comfortable and inclusive environment. With nearly 100 student clubs and organizations, an active Greek Life, and a bustling recreation center, you won't run out of things to do! Meeting new people and friends will come easy. LIU Post has 15 varsity teams that compete in NCAA athletics and is ranked one of the best Division II athletic programs in the country. In fall 2012 we broke ground on a new stadium.
What the School Says...
LIU Post students have a world of opportunities at their fingertips. Study abroad, honors education, undergraduate research, career services, sports, clubs, and social causes combine with a supportive community dedicated to seeing you succeed and stay on track toward graduation. Our student body consists of a wide range of personalities, cultures, talents, and interests. Together, our community creates an atmosphere that is diverse, challenging, and full of cultural experiences. Our students have a history of being leaders in the classroom and beyond. There are thousands of LIU Post graduates in important positions all over the world, doing exciting work in many fields such as medicine, teaching, business, health care, arts management, and much more!
