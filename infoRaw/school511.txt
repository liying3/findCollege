What the School Says...
At Lynn University you'll quickly realize that we're a university like no other. Since our founding in 1962, we have focused on preparing students for success in the world. Lynn is home to one of the most innovative academic programs anywhere. Our student body is diverse. All styles are welcome. So whether your style is intellectual, athletic, musical, political, high fashion or high tech, you'll be sure to flourish at Lynn in sunny South Florida.


What the School Says...
Lynn University has rolling admission, so you can apply at any time. We do encourage high school students to apply by the priority deadline of March 31. As part of the application, Lynn requires first-year applicants to submit SAT/ACT scores in addition to their transcripts, essay, and application fee. International students are also required to submit TOEFL scores. 


What the School Says...
Lynn University's goal is to be affordable. Our knowledgeable and friendly financial aid staff is available to help students find the scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study opportunities needed to make their college dreams a reality. Lynn can be the right "fit" financially. 


What the School Says...
Lynn University offers a school for every interest, passion, and style. With four colleges, two schools, and 24 majors you'll be able to uncover your true academic interests. We'll help you develop the skills and goals to prepare you for your career. Just see where our alums are working. You'll find them at companies like Apple, BMW, Dow Jones, and MTV Latin America. If you're looking to take your education to the next level, our master's degree programs are available on campus or online. Lynn also offers an Ed.D. program in Educational Leadership.


What the School Says...
Lynn University's campus is a sunny and vibrant place where there's always something to do. Our modern residence halls allow students to feel at home and really make the space their own. Through our student organizations and clubs you'll be sure to gain leadership skills and lifelong friendships. To explore life off campus, you won't have to go far. Boca Raton and the neighboring cities of Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Delray Beach, and Palm Beach offer a wide variety of restaurants, festivals, concerts, parks, professional sports, shopping, and entertainment.


What the School Says...
At Lynn University, lifestyle is somewhat of an art form. (Blame it on our gorgeous location and incredibly diverse community.) The bottom line is this: you won't find a more stimulating, fascinating student lifestyle anywhere. Lynn students like to do their own thing (with style, of course).  We're home to one of the most international student bodies in the country and are consistently ranked among the top schools for the number of students who study abroad. Dust off your passport (or make sure you have one). You'll need it!
