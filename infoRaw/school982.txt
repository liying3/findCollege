What the School Says...
The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign is one of the nation's top public universities. Our long tradition of academic excellence drives our dedication to prepare students for their future employment and leadership positions. We offer more than 150 majors and 1,000 student organizations for our 32,000 undergraduate students. You'll find where you belong at Illinois. 


What the School Says...
Admission is selective. We perform a holistic review of your application to form a complete picture of you and how you can benefit from being a part of Illinois. In an admission decision, we consider coursework, academic performance, class rank, ACT and/or SAT scores, application essays, extracurricular activities, volunteer and work experience, and awards and honors.


What the School Says...
Undergraduate students at Illinois are guaranteed the same tuition rate for four continuous academic years. Families should include individual anticipated travel and personal expenses when determining cost to attend college. Note that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security requires international students to certify financial resources for their educational expenses in the U.S. There is no extra application to apply for scholarships at Illinois. You will be considered for all scholarships when you apply for admission.


What the School Says...
Illinois provides a 140-year tradition of academic excellence and success. By learning from faculty members who are among the best in their fields, participating in undergraduate research, and gaining hands-on experience through interning, Illinois students receive an education second to none.

Illinois offers over 150 majors within nine undergraduate academic communities. Freshmen may enter as undeclared. You'll find resources here that will make the difference in your collegiate experience: one of the largest libraries in the nation, state-of-the-art academic and research facilities, the ninth largest study abroad program in the nation, and additional assistance and mentoring programs.




What the School Says...
You'll find the quintessential collegiate experience at Illinois. The large campus offers unlimited opportunities to get involved, and includes more than 1,000 student organizations, from academic to athletic and from philanthropic to fun. 

Illinois has a long tradition as a Big Ten university with 21 varsity sports teams. Cheer with other fans as a member of Illini Pride at historic Memorial Stadium or State Farm Center. Or join a team: There are 40 club sports teams, and Campus Recreation organizes over 20 intramural sports for students. Nine state-of-the-art recreation centers, both indoor and outdoor, are located throughout the campus, including an ice rink, pools, water slides, and a climbing wall. 

Illinois is also known for its live music and theatre scenes, diverse population, and ability to draw national acts. Plus, the surrounding micro-urban community of Champaign-Urbana provides great selection for shopping and dining. 



What the School Says...
You won't find a better value than Illinois. You'll have job certainty, as Illinois undergraduates are rated third in the nation by recruiters (Wall Street Journal's "Top 25 Recruiter Picks"). You'll have a strong salary, as Illinois graduates are top in the Big Ten for mid-career median salaries (PayScale 2012-13 College Salary Report). It's a smart investment, as Illinois is rated first in the Big Ten for best return on investment in 2013 (Wall Street Journal). You'll be prepared for life after your bachelor's degree. The Career Center hosts 27,000-plus interviews on campus each year and in the most recent graduate survey, 65.2 percent of grads were employed full time while 35 percent had enrolled in a post-secondary degree program.
