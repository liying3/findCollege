What the School Says...
Johnson and Wales University (JWU), founded in 1914, is a nonprofit, private accredited institution with campuses in Providence, RI.; North Miami, Fla.; Denver, Colo; and Charlotte, N.C.  At our Charlotte campus students enjoy southern charm in a modern setting with a wealth of internship and career opportunities. Charlotte's plethora of sporting, entertainment, and event offerings are easy for students to enjoy, while students on campus congregate in the newly opened 30,000 square-foot student center. 


What the School Says...
Johnson & Wales University offers rolling admissions with no application fee or deadline, but you are advised to apply as early as possible before your intended date of enrollment for full consideration.  SAT or ACT scores are not required as part of the application but may be required for scholarship consideration or honors program admission.   


What the School Says...
Johnson & Wales University is committed to affordable education with most of its students receiving some type of financial assistance. International students are not eligible for U.S. federal financial aid, but may be awarded academic-based merit scholarships ranging from $1,000 - $5,000 annually. 


What the School Says...
Johnson & Wales University offers more than 40 programs of study throughout its four campuses in Providence, North Miami, Denver, and Charlotte. We offer undergraduate, graduate, and continuing education courses, as well as accelerated programs and online courses. We emphasize practical education in arts and sciences, business, culinary arts, education, hospitality, nutrition, and technology. Our unique educational model features small, professionally focused classes which are taught by accomplished and well-credentialed faculty, many with industry experience.  Students gain valuable real-world experience through directed work study programs, community service projects, relevant internships with more than 1,700 sites worldwide, and over 40 study abroad and exchange program opportunities.


What the School Says...
At JWU, campus life and community thrives at each of our four campuses with a variety of housing options (both on- and off-campus) and a plethora of student activities, clubs, and organizations to join.  Each campus provides its own blend of culture and events, from music and professional sports to recreation, shopping, and dining.  Our Providence campus is sprawling with a historic urban center and Narragansett Bay acreage. Our Charlotte campus is a charming, close knit community with modern facilities. North Miami offers the best of year-round weather with international flair, while Denver offers a traditional campus with an expansive outdoor commons.  


What the School Says...
Johnson & Wales University provides ambitious, purposeful students with the foundation to flourish personally and professionally.  Our university is a student-centered culture with excellent, relevant educational programs focused to prepare students for success.  We offer endless opportunities for student involvement on campus, as well as off campus to fully integrate students throughout the community and workplace.  Students are able to begin courses in their major during their freshman year, helping to ensure they are on the right career track.  With our campuses enrolling students from more than 90 countries and 50 states, our students benefit from diverse perspectives, global connections and an extensive alumni network.
