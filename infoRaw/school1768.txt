What the School Says...
Excellence. Faith. Leadership. Service. These are the pillars of our mission and the foundation of your experience as a Marquette student. Since 1881 we have educated students to become leaders in their professions, to act ethically, to innovate, to Be the Difference.




What the School Says...
Successful applicants to Marquette have challenged themselves academically and have done well in their courses. In the admission review, we highly value service and leadership along with academics.



What the School Says...
We know a Marquette education is an investment in your future. We're proud that we have been named a national "Best Buy in Private Education" and among the top colleges in Wisconsin for the return on your investment. 


What the School Says...
Marquette offers nearly 80 majors in seven colleges. All students begin their studies in their major from day one. Students can select from a wide variety of study abroad, service learning, and accelerated degree programs. They may pursue studies at the Les Aspin Center for Government in Washington, D.C., or with Marquette's programs in Madrid and South Africa, or more deeply integrate classroom work with internship and co-op experiences in Milwaukee and throughout the world.


What the School Says...
More than 96 percent of students choose to live on campus, which is a testament to our strong residential community and the number of opportunities to pursue. Join one of the more than 270 student organizations, compete in nearly 50 club and intramural sports, and explore the city with free, unlimited use of Milwaukee County Transit. 


What the School Says...
 Marquette students come from a variety of backgrounds, beliefs, and experiences. We think this helps to enhance our educational experience. Our retention and graduation rates exceed our peer institutions. And, the National Survey of Student Engagement found that Marquette students outperform their peers in every category measured. Who thrives at Marquette?  Students who actively engage the world, immerse themselves in education, and strive to make our community a better place. Oh, and you have to have a little fun along the way.
