What the School Says...
Founded in 1883, Stetson University, Florida's first private university, has established a tradition of academic excellence. Stetson offers graduate and undergraduate degrees in four schools: The College of Arts and Sciences, The School of Music, The School of Business Administration, and The College of Law. At Stetson University, we are committed to rigorous academics, to the kind of learning that reaches deep inside you, fostering the ability to think critically, reason empirically, express persuasively, and connect creatively across life.  It's active learning that unlocks the power of your potential.


What the School Says...
Stetson University values academic achievement and a commitment to personal values, leadership, talent, character, and initiative. It's smart to apply early. After all, there's a lot of competition to get into a top-ranked school like Stetson, and you'll want to get ahead of the pack! Submission of standardized test results for admission is optional at Stetson. Score-optional consideration is an alternative for applicants who feel that their test scores don't adequately reflect their level of academic achievement or accurately predict their potential. However, if you feel that your test scores are a good reflection of your ability, send them to the Office of Admission and they will be considered along with your other supporting materials. 


What the School Says...
Stetson University is dedicated to making a top-ranked Stetson education accessible for those who dare to be significant. Our financial aid experts will provide you with real options. In fact, they will craft a customized financial aid package just for you and your individual situation. At Stetson, you are not alone. In addition to creating the best possible financial aid package for you, we will give you personal attention and support, not only throughout the admission process, but also throughout your Stetson career.



What the School Says...
At Stetson, we are focused on creating an environment where each student can grow holistically. Learning happens both inside and outside of the classroom. Our 12:1 student-faculty ratio is the foundation for a significant education, ensuring that Stetson faculty take a personal interest in fostering an environment of active learning. So when you come here, regardless of what you choose to study, you will experience more than you ever imagined.  After all, you should enjoy your pursuit of significance.  


What the School Says...
Stetson competes in 18 NCAA Division I sports in the Atlantic Sun Conference and the Pioneer Football League. Stetson also offers vibrant club sports and campus-based intramural programs in 18 different sports. With over 100 active student organizations on campus, there is something for every interest, and all clubs are student-organized and student-led.  If you don't see a club for your interest, you can easily start your own!


What the School Says...
From prestigious graduate schools like Oxford University and Johns Hopkins University to amazing careers in the public and private sectors, Stetson graduates are taking success to new levels.  What's more significant are their achievements.  You can find Hatters working on Capitol Hill, within companies like Google and Disney, performing on Broadway, and serving across the globe.
